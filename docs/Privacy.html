Privacy Policy for PlanTogether

Last updated: February 14, 2026

PlanTogether helps groups plan events. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: Apple Sign In user identifier, and name/email if provided by Apple.
  • App content: Pods, members, proposals, votes, RSVPs, comments, and activity history you create.
  • Optional permissions: Calendar access (to add events), Contacts access (for inviting), notifications, and optional location-based reminders.

2. How We Use Information

  • To run core app features (sync, invitations, planning, reminders).
  • To store and sync your data securely through Apple CloudKit.
  • To improve reliability and fix issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Your data is shared only with:

  • Members of pods you participate in (for collaboration).
  • Apple services required to operate the app (e.g., CloudKit, Sign in with Apple).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on-device and/or in Apple CloudKit using Apple’s security controls. We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete content in-app and request account-related deletion.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke app permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can sign out and stop cloud sync.
  • You can request deletion of your data (see contact below).

7. Children’s Privacy

PlanTogether is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: privacy@plantogether.app
